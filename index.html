Coachella Pitch

TouchDesigner & Adobe XD

I pitched an idea for Coachella to my professors for my Digital Exploration Final. What my pitch was about was a two buildings showing a full 360 degree panorama view using short throw projectors and people from Choachella can "explore" new places while they are still in Coachella. It will have a Phone VR intergration that people can immerse themselves to the place that is showing. There will be two different places that will be showing in the two buildings and it updates every two hours. It will be using real time data using cameras and projectors so it will be able to show onto the walls. Below I attached videos of my proof of concept that I made using TouchDesigner and two projectors that show one output using a webcam. Also another video in how the VR will look like using Adobe XD and my final video pitching the idea.
